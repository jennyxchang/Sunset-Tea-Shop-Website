Our story at Sunset Tea Shop...


Sunset Tea Shop was established on May 31th, 2020, in Corvallis, Oregon. It was founded by three friends who love to drink tea together. The idea of a tea shop sprouted during one of their tea sessions. They have always wanted to share their love for tea with their community. They also wanted to provide their community with a place to relax and socialize with friends and family.

Voila! Sunset Tea Shop was born!

The name Sunset came from the founders always having tea while looking at sunset.

The history and benefits of tea...

For thousands of years, people from different cultures have consumed tea. There are many varieties of tea, such as black tea, green tea, white tea, herbal tea, and many more.

There are numerous benifits from drinking tea. Studies have shown that tea contains antioxidants that may help with cancer, heart disease, diabetes, weight loss, cholesterol, and mental alertness. Studies also found tea to have some antimicrobial effects.
